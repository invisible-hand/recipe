Introduction

Mashed potatoes are a classic comfort food that pairs well with a variety of dishes. This recipe will guide you through making creamy and delicious mashed potatoes that are perfect for any meal.

Ingredients

Ingredients Amount
Potatoes 2 lbs
Butter 1/2 cup
Milk 1/2 cup
Salt To taste
Pepper To taste

Cooking Time

Cooking Time: 30 minutes

Instructions

1. Prepare the Potatoes

Peel the potatoes and cut them into evenly sized chunks.

2. Boil the Potatoes

Place the potatoes in a large pot and cover them with water. Bring the water to a boil and cook the potatoes until they are fork-tender, about 15-20 minutes.

3. Mash the Potatoes

Drain the potatoes and return them to the pot. Add butter, milk, salt, and pepper to the pot. Use a potato masher or a hand mixer to mash the potatoes until they reach your desired consistency.

4. Serve

Transfer the mashed potatoes to a serving dish and garnish with additional butter or herbs, if desired. Serve hot and enjoy!
