Recipe for mashed potato

Introduction:

Making mashed potatoes is a classic comfort food that pairs well with a variety of dishes. This recipe will guide you through creating creamy and delicious mashed potatoes that are perfect for any meal.

Ingredients:

For the mashed potatoes:

- 2 pounds of potatoes (such as russet or Yukon gold) - 1/2 cup of milk - 1/4 cup of butter - Salt and pepper to taste

Cooking Time:

- Preparation Time: 15 minutes - Cooking Time: 20 minutes - Total Time: 35 minutes

Instructions:

1. Prepare the potatoes:

- Peel the potatoes and cut them into evenly sized chunks. - Place the potatoes in a large pot and cover them with cold water. - Add a pinch of salt to the water.

2. Cook the potatoes:

- Bring the pot of water to a boil over medium-high heat. - Reduce the heat to low and simmer the potatoes for about 15-20 minutes, or until they are fork-tender.

3. Mash the potatoes:

- Drain the potatoes and return them to the pot. - Add the butter and milk to the pot. - Use a potato masher or a hand mixer to mash the potatoes until they reach your desired consistency. - Season with salt and pepper to taste.

4. Serve:

- Transfer the mashed potatoes to a serving dish. - Garnish with a pat of butter or a sprinkle of chopped parsley, if desired. - Serve hot and enjoy!
