Recipe for green veggie smoothie

Introduction:
Start your day off right with this refreshing and nutritious green veggie smoothie. Packed with vitamins and minerals, this smoothie is a great way to sneak in some extra greens into your diet.

Ingredients:
- 1 cup spinach
- 1/2 cucumber, peeled and chopped
- 1/2 avocado
- 1 green apple, cored and chopped
- 1/2 lemon, juiced
- 1 cup coconut water
- Ice cubes (optional)

Cooking time:
5 minutes

Instructions:
1. In a blender, combine the spinach, cucumber, avocado, green apple, lemon juice, and coconut water.
2. Blend on high until smooth and creamy.
3. If desired, add ice cubes and blend again until smooth.
4. Pour into a glass and enjoy immediately.

Feel free to customize this recipe by adding in other green veggies like kale or celery, or sweetening it with a touch of honey or maple syrup. Cheers to a healthy start to your day!
