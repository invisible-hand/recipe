Introduction

This recipe will guide you through making a delicious homemade hamburger that is perfect for a quick and satisfying meal.

Ingredients

Ingredient Amount
Ground beef 1 pound
Onion 1, finely chopped
Garlic 2 cloves, minced
Worcestershire sauce 1 tablespoon
Salt 1 teaspoon
Pepper 1/2 teaspoon
Hamburger buns 4
Optional toppings (lettuce, tomato, cheese, etc.) To taste

Cooking Time

Total: 20 minutes

Instructions

  1. Prepare the patties: In a mixing bowl, combine the ground beef, chopped onion, minced garlic, Worcestershire sauce, salt, and pepper. Mix well until all ingredients are evenly distributed.
  2. Form the patties: Divide the mixture into 4 equal portions and shape them into patties of your desired thickness.
  3. Cook the patties: Heat a skillet or grill over medium-high heat. Cook the patties for about 4-5 minutes on each side, or until they reach your preferred level of doneness.
  4. Assemble the burgers: Toast the hamburger buns lightly. Place a cooked patty on the bottom half of each bun. Add your favorite toppings, such as lettuce, tomato, cheese, and condiments.
  5. Serve: Put the top half of the bun on each burger and serve hot. Enjoy your homemade hamburgers!
