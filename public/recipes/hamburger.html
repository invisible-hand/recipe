Recipe for hamburger

Introduction:
Hamburgers are a classic American dish that is loved by people of all ages. This recipe will show you how to make a delicious and juicy hamburger that is perfect for a summer barbecue or a quick weeknight dinner.

Ingredients:
- 1 pound of ground beef
- 1 teaspoon of salt
- 1/2 teaspoon of black pepper
- 1/2 teaspoon of garlic powder
- 1/2 teaspoon of onion powder
- 4 hamburger buns
- Optional toppings: lettuce, tomato, onion, cheese, ketchup, mustard, mayonnaise

Cooking time:
- Prep time: 10 minutes
- Cook time: 10 minutes
- Total time: 20 minutes

Instructions:
1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the ground beef, salt, pepper, garlic powder, and onion powder. Mix well until all the seasonings are evenly distributed throughout the meat.
2. Divide the meat mixture into 4 equal portions and shape each portion into a patty that is about 1/2 inch thick.
3. Preheat a grill or a skillet over medium-high heat. Place the hamburger patties on the grill or skillet and cook for about 4-5 minutes on each side, or until the internal temperature reaches 160Â°F.
4. While the patties are cooking, toast the hamburger buns on the grill or in a toaster oven until they are lightly browned.
5. Once the patties are cooked, remove them from the grill or skillet and let them rest for a few minutes.
6. Assemble the hamburgers by placing a patty on the bottom half of each bun. Add your desired toppings, such as lettuce, tomato, onion, cheese, ketchup, mustard, and mayonnaise. Top with the other half of the bun.
7. Serve the hamburgers hot and enjoy!
