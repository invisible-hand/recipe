hamburger

*Introduction:* A classic American favorite, the hamburger is a delicious and satisfying meal that can be customized to suit your taste. Whether you prefer it with cheese, bacon, or just the classic toppings, this recipe will guide you through creating the perfect hamburger.

Ingredients:

Ingredient Amount
Ground beef 1 pound
Salt 1 teaspoon
Black pepper 1/2 teaspoon
Hamburger buns 4
Cheese slices 4
Lettuce 4 leaves
Tomato 1, sliced
Onion 1, sliced

Cooking Time:

Preparation time:

10 minutes

Cooking time:

10 minutes

Instructions:

  1. Preheat a grill or skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. In a bowl, season the ground beef with salt and black pepper. Mix well.
  3. Divide the seasoned beef into 4 equal portions and shape them into patties.
  4. Place the patties on the grill or skillet and cook for about 4-5 minutes per side, or until desired doneness.
  5. During the last minute of cooking, place a slice of cheese on each patty to melt.
  6. Toast the hamburger buns on the grill or skillet for about 1 minute.
  7. Assemble the hamburgers by placing a patty on the bottom bun, then topping with lettuce, tomato, and onion. Add the top bun and serve hot.

Tips:

  • For a juicier burger, avoid pressing down on the patties while cooking to retain the juices.
  • Experiment with different toppings such as bacon, avocado, or pickles to customize your hamburger.
  • Make sure to toast the buns to add a nice crunch and prevent them from getting soggy.
