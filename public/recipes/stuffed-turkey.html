Stuffed Turkey Recipe

Ingredients:

Ingredient Quantity
Turkey 1 (12-14 lbs)
Butter 1/2 cup
Onion 1, chopped
Celery 2 stalks, chopped
Carrots 2, chopped
Garlic 4 cloves, minced
Bread cubes 4 cups
Chicken broth 2 cups
Salt To taste
Pepper To taste

Cooking Time:

Total Time: 4 hours

Cook Time: 3 hours

Prep Time: 1 hour

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 325°F (165°C).
  2. Rinse the turkey inside and out, pat dry with paper towels, and season with salt and pepper.
  3. In a skillet, melt butter and sauté onion, celery, carrots, and garlic until softened.
  4. Remove from heat and stir in bread cubes and chicken broth until well combined.
  5. Stuff the turkey cavity with the bread mixture and truss the turkey with kitchen twine.
  6. Place the stuffed turkey on a roasting pan and cover with foil.
  7. Roast in the preheated oven for 3 hours, basting occasionally with pan juices.
  8. Remove the foil during the last 30 minutes of cooking to brown the skin.
  9. Check the internal temperature of the turkey with a meat thermometer (should read 165°F or 75°C).
  10. Let the turkey rest for 20 minutes before carving and serving.
