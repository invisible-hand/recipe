Milkshake with Blueberries

Introduction

Indulge in the creamy delight of a homemade Milkshake with Blueberries. This refreshing treat combines the natural sweetness of ripe blueberries with the rich, smooth texture of ice cream and milk, creating a perfect beverage for any time of the day. Whether you're looking to cool down on a hot summer afternoon or seeking a sweet dessert to cap off your meal, this milkshake recipe is sure to satisfy your cravings.

Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity
Fresh blueberries 1 cup
Vanilla ice cream 2 cups
Milk (whole or 2%) 1/2 cup
Sugar (optional) 1 tablespoon
Vanilla extract 1/2 teaspoon
Whipped cream (for garnish) To taste
Additional blueberries (for garnish) To taste

Cooking Time

Preparation Time: 5 minutes

Total Time: 5 minutes

Instructions

  1. Rinse the blueberries and pat them dry with a paper towel.
  2. Place the blueberries, vanilla ice cream, milk, sugar (if using), and vanilla extract into a blender.
  3. Blend on high speed until the mixture is smooth and creamy. If the shake is too thick, you can add a little more milk to reach your desired consistency.
  4. Taste the milkshake and add more sugar if needed, then blend again briefly to mix.
  5. Pour the milkshake into chilled glasses.
  6. Garnish with a dollop of whipped cream and a few fresh blueberries on top.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy your refreshing Milkshake with Blueberries!
