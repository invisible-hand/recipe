Recipe for cheeseburger

Introduction:
Cheeseburgers are a classic American dish that is loved by many. This recipe will show you how to make a delicious and juicy cheeseburger that is perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients:
- 1 lb ground beef
- Salt and pepper
- 4 slices of cheese (cheddar, American, or your choice)
- 4 hamburger buns
- Lettuce
- Tomato slices
- Onion slices
- Pickles
- Ketchup
- Mustard

Cooking time: 15 minutes

Instructions:
1. Preheat your grill or stovetop skillet over medium-high heat.
2. Divide the ground beef into 4 equal portions and shape them into patties. Season each patty with salt and pepper.
3. Place the patties on the grill or skillet and cook for about 4-5 minutes on each side, or until they reach your desired level of doneness.
4. During the last minute of cooking, place a slice of cheese on top of each patty and allow it to melt.
5. While the patties are cooking, toast the hamburger buns on the grill or in a toaster.
6. Assemble your cheeseburgers by placing a patty with melted cheese on the bottom half of each bun.
7. Top each patty with lettuce, tomato slices, onion slices, and pickles.
8. Spread ketchup and mustard on the top half of each bun.
9. Place the top half of the bun on top of the toppings to complete the cheeseburger.
10. Serve hot and enjoy your delicious homemade cheeseburgers!
