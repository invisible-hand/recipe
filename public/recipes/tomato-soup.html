Recipe for Tomato soup

Introduction:
Tomato soup is a classic comfort food that is perfect for a cozy night in or a quick and easy meal. This recipe is simple to make and bursting with flavor from fresh tomatoes and herbs. Serve it with a side of crusty bread or a grilled cheese sandwich for a satisfying meal.

Ingredients:
- 2 pounds of ripe tomatoes, chopped
- 1 onion, chopped
- 2 cloves of garlic, minced
- 2 tablespoons of olive oil
- 4 cups of vegetable broth
- 1 teaspoon of sugar
- 1 teaspoon of salt
- 1/2 teaspoon of black pepper
- 1/2 teaspoon of dried basil
- 1/2 teaspoon of dried oregano
- 1/4 teaspoon of red pepper flakes (optional)
- 1/2 cup of heavy cream (optional)
- Fresh basil leaves, for garnish

Cooking time: 30 minutes

Instructions:
1. In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and garlic and saut√© until softened, about 5 minutes.
2. Add the chopped tomatoes to the pot and cook for another 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
3. Pour in the vegetable broth and bring the mixture to a simmer. Add the sugar, salt, pepper, basil, oregano, and red pepper flakes, if using. Let the soup simmer for 15-20 minutes, until the tomatoes are soft and the flavors have melded together.
4. Using an immersion blender, blend the soup until smooth. Alternatively, you can transfer the soup to a blender and blend in batches until smooth.
5. Stir in the heavy cream, if using, and adjust the seasoning to taste.
6. Serve the tomato soup hot, garnished with fresh basil leaves. Enjoy!
