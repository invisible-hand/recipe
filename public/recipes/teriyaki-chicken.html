Recipe for teriyaki chicken

Introduction

This teriyaki chicken recipe is a delicious and easy dish that is perfect for a quick weeknight dinner. The sweet and savory flavors of the teriyaki sauce pair perfectly with tender chicken, making it a crowd-pleaser for the whole family.

Ingredients

Ingredients Amount
Chicken thighs 1 lb
For the marinade:
Soy sauce 1/4 cup
Water 1/4 cup
Brown sugar 2 tbsp
Honey 2 tbsp
Garlic, minced 2 cloves
Ginger, grated 1 tsp
Cornstarch 1 tbsp
Green onions, chopped for garnish

Cooking Time

Cooking Time: 30 minutes

Instructions

1. Marinate the chicken:

In a bowl, mix together soy sauce, water, brown sugar, honey, garlic, and ginger to make the marinade. Place the chicken thighs in a resealable plastic bag or shallow dish, and pour the marinade over the chicken. Seal the bag or cover the dish, and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to marinate.

2. Cook the chicken:

Heat a skillet over medium-high heat. Remove the chicken from the marinade, reserving the marinade. Cook the chicken thighs in the skillet for about 6-7 minutes per side, or until cooked through.

3. Make the sauce:

In a small saucepan, bring the reserved marinade to a boil. In a separate bowl, mix cornstarch with a little water to make a slurry. Slowly pour the slurry into the boiling marinade, stirring constantly until the sauce thickens.

4. Serve:

Once the chicken is cooked, brush the teriyaki sauce over the chicken thighs. Serve the teriyaki chicken hot, garnished with chopped green onions. Enjoy!
