Recipe for mushed potato

Introduction:
Mushed potatoes are a classic comfort food that pairs well with a variety of dishes. This recipe for creamy and buttery mashed potatoes is simple to make and sure to be a hit at your next meal.

Ingredients:
- 2 pounds of potatoes (such as russet or Yukon gold)
- 1/2 cup of milk
- 1/4 cup of butter
- Salt and pepper to taste

Cooking time:
Preparation time: 10 minutes
Cooking time: 20 minutes
Total time: 30 minutes

Instructions:
1. Peel the potatoes and cut them into evenly sized chunks. Place them in a large pot and cover with water. Bring to a boil over medium-high heat and cook until the potatoes are fork-tender, about 15-20 minutes.

2. Drain the potatoes and return them to the pot. Add the milk and butter, then mash the potatoes with a potato masher until smooth and creamy. Add more milk if needed to reach your desired consistency.

3. Season the mashed potatoes with salt and pepper to taste, stirring to combine.

4. Serve the mashed potatoes hot as a side dish with your favorite main course. Enjoy!
