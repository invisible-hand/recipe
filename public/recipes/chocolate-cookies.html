Recipe for chocolate cookies

Introduction

Indulge in the sweet delight of homemade chocolate cookies. These cookies are not only delicious but also easy to make. They are perfect for any occasion, whether it's a party, a family gathering, or just a simple snack. Let's get started!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 1/2 cups of white sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons of vanilla extract
  • 2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 2/3 cup of cocoa powder
  • 3/4 teaspoon of baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 2 cups of semisweet chocolate chips

Cooking Time

The total time required for this recipe is 30 minutes. This includes 15 minutes of preparation time and 15 minutes of baking time.

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. In a large bowl, cream together the butter and sugar until smooth. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla extract.
  3. In a separate bowl, combine the flour, cocoa powder, baking soda, and salt. Gradually add this to the butter mixture and mix well.
  4. Finally, fold in the chocolate chips.
