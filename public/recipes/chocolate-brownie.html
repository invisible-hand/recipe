Chocolate Brownie Recipe

Ingredients:

Quantity Ingredient
1 cup Butter
1 3/4 cups Sugar
4 Eggs
1 tsp Vanilla extract
1 cup All-purpose flour
1/2 cup Cocoa powder
1/4 tsp Salt
1/2 cup Chocolate chips

Cooking Time:

Preparation Time: 15 minutes

Cooking Time: 25-30 minutes

Total Time: 40-45 minutes

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a 9x13 inch baking pan.
  2. In a saucepan, melt the butter over low heat. Remove from heat and stir in sugar, eggs, and vanilla extract until well combined.
  3. Stir in the flour, cocoa powder, and salt until just combined. Fold in the chocolate chips.
  4. Pour the batter into the prepared baking pan and spread it evenly.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out with moist crumbs, but not wet batter.
  6. Allow the brownies to cool in the pan before cutting into squares and serving.
