Shortbread Cookie Recipe

Ingredients:

Ingredient Amount
Butter 1 cup (2 sticks), softened
Granulated sugar 1/2 cup
All-purpose flour 2 cups
Salt 1/4 teaspoon

Cooking Time:

Cooking Time: 20 minutes

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (180°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Cream together the softened butter and sugar in a mixing bowl until light and fluffy.
  3. Add the flour and salt to the butter mixture, and mix until a dough forms.
  4. Roll out the dough on a floured surface to about 1/4 inch thickness.
  5. Cut out the shortbread cookies using cookie cutters or a knife, and place them on the prepared baking sheet.
  6. Poke the cookies with a fork to create a decorative pattern.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes, or until the edges are lightly golden.
  8. Cool the cookies on a wire rack before serving.
