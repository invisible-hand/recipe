Baked Fish

Introduction:

This baked fish recipe is a simple and delicious way to enjoy a healthy meal. The fish is seasoned with herbs and spices, then baked to perfection. It's a great option for a quick weeknight dinner or a special occasion.

Ingredients:

Ingredients Amount
Fresh fish fillets (such as salmon or cod) 4 fillets
Garlic powder 1 tsp
Paprika 1 tsp
Dried oregano 1 tsp
Salt 1/2 tsp
Black pepper 1/2 tsp
Extra virgin olive oil 2 tbsp
Lemon 1, sliced

Cooking Time:

Cooking Time: 20 minutes

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Place the fish fillets on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  3. In a small bowl, mix together the garlic powder, paprika, oregano, salt, and black pepper.
  4. Brush the fish fillets with olive oil, then sprinkle the seasoning mixture evenly over the fillets.
  5. Place a slice of lemon on top of each fillet.
  6. Bake the fish in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes, or until the fish is cooked through and flakes easily with a fork.
  7. Serve the baked fish hot with your favorite side dishes and enjoy!
