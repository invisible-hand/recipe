Introduction

Fried chicken wings are a classic and delicious dish that is perfect for any occasion. This recipe will guide you through making crispy and flavorful fried chicken wings that are sure to be a hit with your family and friends.

Ingredients

Ingredients Amount
Chicken wings 2 lbs
All-purpose flour 1 cup
Eggs 2
Milk 1/2 cup
Seasoned salt 1 tsp
Black pepper 1/2 tsp
Garlic powder 1/2 tsp
Vegetable oil for frying

Cooking Time

Cooking Time: 30 minutes

Instructions

1. Prepare the chicken wings

Wash the chicken wings and pat them dry with paper towels. Season the wings with seasoned salt, black pepper, and garlic powder.

2. Coat the chicken wings

In a shallow dish, whisk together the eggs and milk. In another dish, place the flour. Dip each chicken wing into the egg mixture, then coat it with flour.

3. Fry the chicken wings

Heat vegetable oil in a deep fryer or large skillet to 375Â°F. Carefully add the chicken wings to the hot oil and fry for about 10-12 minutes, or until they are golden brown and cooked through.

4. Drain and serve

Remove the fried chicken wings from the oil and place them on a paper towel-lined plate to drain excess oil. Serve hot and enjoy!
