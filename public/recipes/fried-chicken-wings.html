fried chicken wings

*A classic and delicious dish that is perfect for any occasion. These crispy fried chicken wings are sure to be a hit with your family and friends.*

Ingredients

Quantity Ingredient
2 lbs Chicken wings
1 cup All-purpose flour
1 tsp Salt
1 tsp Black pepper
1 tsp Paprika
Vegetable oil For frying

Cooking Time

Preparation time: 10 minutes

Cooking time: 20 minutes

Instructions

  1. Pat dry the chicken wings with paper towels to remove excess moisture.
  2. In a bowl, mix the flour, salt, black pepper, and paprika.
  3. Dredge the chicken wings in the flour mixture, making sure they are evenly coated.
  4. Heat vegetable oil in a deep fryer or large skillet to 375Â°F.
  5. Carefully add the chicken wings to the hot oil and fry until golden brown and cooked through, about 10-12 minutes.
  6. Remove the chicken wings from the oil and place them on a paper towel-lined plate to drain excess oil.
  7. Serve the fried chicken wings hot with your favorite dipping sauce.

Tips

  • For extra crispy wings, double dredge them in the flour mixture before frying.
  • Make sure the oil is at the right temperature before adding the chicken wings to ensure they cook evenly and become crispy.
  • Experiment with different seasonings and spices to customize the flavor of your fried chicken wings.
