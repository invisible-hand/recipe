Recipe for fried chicken wings

Introduction:
Fried chicken wings are a classic comfort food that is perfect for any occasion. Whether you're hosting a party or just craving a tasty snack, these crispy wings are sure to satisfy your cravings. Follow this simple recipe to make delicious fried chicken wings that are crispy on the outside and juicy on the inside.

Ingredients:
- 2 lbs chicken wings
- 1 cup all-purpose flour
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tsp pepper
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 1 tsp paprika
- 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper (optional)
- Vegetable oil for frying

Cooking time:
- Prep time: 10 minutes
- Cook time: 20 minutes
- Total time: 30 minutes

Instructions:
1. In a large bowl, mix together the flour, salt, pepper, garlic powder, paprika, and cayenne pepper (if using).
2. Rinse the chicken wings and pat them dry with paper towels.
3. Dredge the chicken wings in the seasoned flour mixture, making sure to coat them evenly.
4. Heat vegetable oil in a deep fryer or large skillet to 375Â°F.
5. Carefully add the chicken wings to the hot oil, making sure not to overcrowd the pan. Fry the wings in batches if necessary.
6. Fry the chicken wings for about 10-12 minutes, turning occasionally, until they are golden brown and crispy.
7. Remove the wings from the oil and place them on a paper towel-lined plate to drain any excess oil.
8. Serve the fried chicken wings hot with your favorite dipping sauce or enjoy them as is.
9. Enjoy your delicious homemade fried chicken wings!
