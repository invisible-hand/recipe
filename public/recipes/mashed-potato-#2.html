Recipe for mashed potato #2

Introduction

This recipe for mashed potatoes is a classic side dish that is creamy, buttery, and perfect for any meal. Whether you're serving it with roast chicken, steak, or just on its own, these mashed potatoes are sure to be a hit!

Ingredients

Ingredients Amount
Potatoes 2 lbs
Butter 1/2 cup
Milk 1/2 cup
Salt To taste
Pepper To taste

Cooking Time

Cooking Time: 30 minutes

Instructions

1. Prepare the Potatoes

Peel the potatoes and cut them into evenly sized chunks.

2. Boil the Potatoes

Place the potatoes in a large pot and cover them with water. Bring the water to a boil and cook the potatoes until they are fork-tender, about 15-20 minutes.

3. Mash the Potatoes

Drain the potatoes and return them to the pot. Add the butter, milk, salt, and pepper. Use a potato masher to mash the potatoes until they reach your desired consistency.

4. Serve

Transfer the mashed potatoes to a serving dish and serve hot. Enjoy!
