Homemade Pasta with Tomatoes

Ingredients:

Ingredient Amount
Pasta (of your choice) 1 lb
Tomatoes 4 large, diced
Garlic 3 cloves, minced
Onion 1 medium, chopped
Olive oil 2 tbsp
Fresh basil 1/4 cup, chopped
Salt To taste
Black pepper To taste

Cooking Time:

Total Time: 30 minutes

Instructions:

  1. Cook the Pasta: Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the pasta and cook according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. Prepare the Sauce: In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add the garlic and onion, saut√© until fragrant and translucent.
  3. Add Tomatoes: Add the diced tomatoes to the skillet and cook until they start to break down and release their juices.
  4. Season: Season the sauce with salt and black pepper to taste. Let it simmer for a few minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
  5. Combine: Add the cooked pasta to the skillet with the tomato sauce. Toss to coat the pasta evenly with the sauce.
  6. Serve: Remove from heat, sprinkle with fresh basil, and serve hot. Enjoy your homemade pasta with tomatoes!
