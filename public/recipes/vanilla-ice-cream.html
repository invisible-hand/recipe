Vanilla Ice Cream

A classic and creamy dessert that never fails to satisfy your sweet tooth. This homemade vanilla ice cream recipe will have you coming back for more!

Ingredients

Ingredient Amount
Milk 2 cups
Heavy cream 1 cup
Sugar 3/4 cup
Vanilla extract 1 tablespoon

Cooking Time

Preparation Time:

10 minutes

Cooking Time:

30 minutes

Total Time:

40 minutes

Instructions

  1. In a saucepan, heat the milk and heavy cream over medium heat until it just begins to simmer.
  2. Remove from heat and stir in the sugar until dissolved.
  3. Stir in the vanilla extract.
  4. Transfer the mixture to a bowl and let it cool to room temperature.
  5. Once cooled, cover the bowl and refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight.
  6. Pour the chilled mixture into an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer's instructions.
  7. Transfer the churned ice cream to a container and freeze for at least 2 hours before serving.

Tips

  • For a richer flavor, you can use vanilla bean instead of vanilla extract.
  • Feel free to add mix-ins like chocolate chips or nuts for extra texture and flavor.
  • Make sure your ice cream maker bowl is completely frozen before churning the mixture to achieve the best consistency.
