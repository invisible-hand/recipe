Vanilla Ice Cream

Introduction:

This classic vanilla ice cream recipe is perfect for those hot summer days or any time you're craving a sweet treat. It's creamy, smooth, and bursting with vanilla flavor.

Ingredients:

Ingredients Amount
Milk 2 cups
Heavy cream 1 cup
Sugar 3/4 cup
Salt 1/4 tsp
Egg yolks 4
Vanilla extract 2 tsp

Cooking Time:

Cooking Time: 30 minutes

Instructions:

  1. In a saucepan, heat the milk, heavy cream, sugar, and salt over medium heat until it just begins to simmer.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk the egg yolks until they are pale and slightly thickened.
  3. Slowly pour the hot milk mixture into the egg yolks, whisking constantly to temper the eggs.
  4. Pour the mixture back into the saucepan and cook over low heat, stirring constantly, until the mixture thickens and coats the back of a spoon.
  5. Remove from heat and stir in the vanilla extract.
  6. Strain the mixture through a fine-mesh sieve into a bowl and let it cool to room temperature.
  7. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap, pressing it directly onto the surface of the custard to prevent a skin from forming.
  8. Refrigerate the custard for at least 4 hours or overnight.
  9. Pour the custard into an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer's instructions.
  10. Transfer the churned ice cream to a container and freeze for at least 4 hours before serving.

Tips:

  • For a richer flavor, you can add a vanilla bean to the milk mixture while heating.
  • Make sure the custard is completely chilled before churning to ensure a creamy texture.
  • Feel free to add mix-ins like chocolate chips, nuts, or fruit to customize your vanilla ice cream.
