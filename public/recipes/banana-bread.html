Recipe for Banana bread

Introduction:
Banana bread is a classic and delicious treat that is perfect for breakfast or as a snack. This recipe is easy to make and will fill your kitchen with the warm and comforting aroma of freshly baked bread.

Ingredients:
- 2-3 ripe bananas, mashed
- 1/3 cup melted butter
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- Pinch of salt
- 3/4 cup sugar
- 1 large egg, beaten
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour

Cooking time:
Prep time: 10 minutes
Cook time: 1 hour
Total time: 1 hour 10 minutes

Instructions:
1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a 9x5 inch loaf pan.
2. In a large mixing bowl, mash the ripe bananas with a fork until smooth.
3. Stir in the melted butter, baking soda, salt, sugar, beaten egg, and vanilla extract until well combined.
4. Gradually add in the flour and mix until just combined. Be careful not to overmix the batter.
5. Pour the batter into the prepared loaf pan and smooth the top with a spatula.
6. Bake in the preheated oven for 1 hour, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
7. Allow the banana bread to cool in the pan for 10 minutes before transferring it to a wire rack to cool completely.
8. Slice and enjoy your delicious homemade banana bread!
