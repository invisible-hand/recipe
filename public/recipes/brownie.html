Recipe for brownie

Introduction

This brownie recipe is perfect for satisfying your chocolate cravings. These brownies are rich, fudgy, and incredibly delicious. They are easy to make and will surely impress your friends and family.

Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity
Butter 1/2 cup
Sugar 1 cup
Eggs 2
Vanilla extract 1 tsp
All-purpose flour 1/3 cup
Cocoa powder 1/4 cup
Salt 1/4 tsp

Cooking Time

Cooking time: 25-30 minutes

Instructions

1. Preheat the oven

Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a 9x9 inch baking pan.

2. Melt the butter

In a microwave-safe bowl, melt the butter. Stir in the sugar until well combined.

3. Add eggs and vanilla

Beat in the eggs, one at a time, then stir in the vanilla extract.

4. Mix dry ingredients

In a separate bowl, combine the flour, cocoa powder, and salt. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined.

5. Bake

Pour the batter into the prepared baking pan and spread it evenly. Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out with a few moist crumbs.

6. Cool and serve

Allow the brownies to cool in the pan before cutting into squares and serving. Enjoy your delicious homemade brownies!
