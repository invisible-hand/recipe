Recipe for vegeterian lasagna

Introduction

Welcome to a delightful journey into the world of vegetarian cuisine with our Vegetarian Lasagna recipe. This dish is a perfect blend of hearty vegetables, rich tomato sauce, and creamy cheese, all layered between tender sheets of pasta. It's a comforting, nutritious, and satisfying meal that's sure to please both vegetarians and meat-lovers alike. Whether you're cooking for a family dinner, a potluck, or simply prepping meals for the week, this vegetarian lasagna is a fantastic choice that's packed with flavor and goodness.

Ingredients

For the Vegetable Filling

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 zucchini, diced
  • 1 bell pepper, any color, diced
  • 1 small eggplant, diced
  • 1 cup spinach, fresh or frozen
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

For the Tomato Sauce

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Additional Ingredients

  • 9-12 no-boil lasagna noodles
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

Cooking Time

Preparation Time: 30 minutes

Cooking Time: 45 minutes

Total Time: 1 hour 15 minutes
