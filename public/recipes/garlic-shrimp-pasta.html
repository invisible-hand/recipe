Recipe for garlic shrimp pasta

Introduction:

This garlic shrimp pasta recipe is a delicious and easy dish that combines succulent shrimp with flavorful garlic and pasta. It's perfect for a quick weeknight dinner or a special occasion.

Ingredients:

Main Ingredients:

  • 1 pound shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 8 oz pasta of your choice
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Optional Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup white wine
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Red pepper flakes for a spicy kick

Cooking Time:

Total: Approximately 20 minutes

Instructions:

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil and cook the pasta according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and cook until fragrant, about 1 minute.
  3. Add the shrimp to the skillet and cook until pink and opaque, about 2-3 minutes per side.
  4. If using white wine, pour it into the skillet and let it simmer for 1-2 minutes.
  5. Add the cooked pasta to the skillet and toss to combine with the shrimp and garlic.
  6. Season with salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes if desired. Stir in the chopped parsley.
  7. Remove from heat and sprinkle with grated Parmesan cheese before serving.
  8. Enjoy your delicious garlic shrimp pasta!
