Recipe for bolognese pasta

Introduction

This bolognese pasta recipe is a classic Italian dish that is hearty, flavorful, and perfect for a cozy night in. The rich meat sauce pairs perfectly with al dente pasta, making it a comforting and satisfying meal.

Ingredients

Ingredient Amount
Ground beef 1 lb
Onion, diced 1
Carrot, diced 1
Celery, diced 1 stalk
Garlic, minced 3 cloves
Canned tomatoes, crushed 28 oz
Tomato paste 2 tbsp
Red wine 1/2 cup
Beef broth 1 cup
Dried oregano 1 tsp
Dried basil 1 tsp
Salt and pepper To taste
Olive oil 2 tbsp
Pasta 1 lb
Grated Parmesan cheese For serving

Cooking Time

Total: 1 hour 30 minutes

Prep: 15 minutes

Cook: 1 hour 15 minutes

Instructions

1. Brown the Ground Beef

In a large pot or Dutch oven, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add the ground beef and cook until browned, breaking it up with a spoon as it cooks.

2. Add Vegetables

Add the diced onion, carrot, celery, and minced garlic to the pot. Cook until the vegetables are softened, about 5 minutes.

3. Make the Sauce

Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, red wine, beef broth, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Bring the sauce to a simmer, then reduce the heat to low and let it simmer for 1 hour, stirring occasionally.

4. Cook the Pasta

While the sauce is simmering, cook the pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain the pasta and set aside.

5. Serve

Once the sauce has thickened and the flavors have melded together, serve the bolognese sauce over the cooked pasta. Top with grated Parmesan cheese and enjoy!
