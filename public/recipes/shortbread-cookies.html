Shortbread Cookies

Introduction

These buttery and crumbly shortbread cookies are a classic treat that is perfect for any occasion. With just a few simple ingredients, you can whip up a batch of these delicious cookies in no time.

Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity
Butter 1 cup (2 sticks), softened
Granulated sugar 1/2 cup
All-purpose flour 2 cups
Cornstarch 1/4 cup
Salt 1/4 tsp

Cooking Time

Preparation: 15 minutes Cooking: 20 minutes Total: 35 minutes

Instructions

1. Preheat the oven

- Preheat your oven to 325°F (160°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

2. Cream the butter and sugar

- In a mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter and granulated sugar until light and fluffy.

3. Add dry ingredients

- Sift in the all-purpose flour, cornstarch, and salt into the butter mixture. Mix until a dough forms.

4. Shape the dough

- Roll out the dough on a floured surface to about 1/4 inch thickness. Use cookie cutters to cut out shapes or simply slice the dough into squares.

5. Bake the cookies

- Place the cookies on the prepared baking sheet and bake in the preheated oven for 18-20 minutes, or until the edges are lightly golden.

6. Cool and enjoy

- Allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely. Enjoy your delicious shortbread cookies!
