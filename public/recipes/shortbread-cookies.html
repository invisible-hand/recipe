Shortbread Cookies

Shortbread cookies are a classic treat that are perfect for any occasion. These buttery, crumbly cookies are a delight to enjoy with a cup of tea or coffee. Plus, they are incredibly easy to make, so you can whip up a batch in no time. Just be careful, they are so delicious that they might disappear before you know it! Now, let's get baking!

Ingredients

Ingredient Amount
Butter 1 cup (2 sticks), softened
Granulated sugar 1/2 cup
All-purpose flour 2 cups
Salt 1/4 teaspoon

Cooking Time

Prep time: 15 minutes

Cook time: 20 minutes

Total time: 35 minutes

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (180°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter and granulated sugar until light and fluffy.
  3. Add the flour and salt to the bowl and mix until a dough forms.
  4. Roll out the dough on a floured surface to about 1/4 inch thickness.
  5. Use cookie cutters to cut out shapes from the dough and place them on the prepared baking sheet.
  6. Bake the cookies in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes, or until the edges are lightly golden.
  7. Remove from the oven and let the cookies cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.
  8. Enjoy your delicious homemade shortbread cookies!

Tips

  • For a twist, you can add a teaspoon of vanilla extract or lemon zest to the dough for extra flavor.
  • Make sure not to overmix the dough, as this can result in tough cookies.
  • Store the cookies in an airtight container to keep them fresh for longer.
